Become part of Waste Kompost Radio!

We are looking for sonic contributions to create a new radio in October 2022 which will only play discarded material. Radios, composers, artists from around the world are invited to become part of this collective radio by sending their unwanted “sound waste”, everything which couldn’t be included in the final result, wrong or useless and therefore cut out. All the waste will be collected to create WKR - Waste Kompost Radio.

WKR will be a constant radio flow composed of sonic waste discarded during editing processes. During the year, the collected waste will be processed through algorithms which gradually decompose the sonic information through applying the same mechanisms of biological and chemical reactions which decompose organic material into compost.

In order to collect sonic waste, WKR is launching several calls for contributions throughout the year. The sonic waste from each call will be collected in a database and activated into its compost-process at the official launch of the radio on 21 October 2022, Pro Helvetia residency NEW ECHO SYSTEMS in Palazzo Trevisan in Venice.

How to throw sonic waste away to WKR:

You can upload your sonic waste directly by clicking on the green floating button.
If instead you have physical sonic waste to send (cassettes, vinyl, cds,...) please address it to:

What kind of sonic waste can you throw into the compost?

All kinds of discarded sounds. Central to the concept is your choice of considering the sound not good for your final result. They can be from:
- radio programmes
- music recording sessions
- music post productions
- field recordings
- general sound recordings (interviews, phone calls, sighs, inappropriate laughter, ehms, ...)
- ...
These are just some examples. Feel free to send us whatever was not fit.
Formats: mp3, wav, flac, ogg, and anything that digitally plays.

When do you need to send your sonic waste in?

The lid of the first compost opens on the occasion of Radiophrenia 2022 in Glasgow: 7 February 2022, and closes on the end date of SonOhr Festival in Bern: 27 February 2022, 23:59 along the 180° meridian.

What will happen to your waste?

It will be collected into a database, the main compost structure from where the compost-algorithms will elaborate it and you will hear how it will be processed on WKR, starting from the 21 October 2022. We will also add your name and the title of your contribution to our website, as part of the community contributing to WKR.
